what do you want to write about?

Each sub-mode will give you a question to answer in your journal entry.

one word prompt

This sub-mode will ask you to write a story about a given word.

a few words prompt

This sub-mode will ask you to write a story that includes each word of a given list of words.

paragraph prompt

In this sub-mode you will get a paragraph. It is the beginning of a story, feel free to continue writing on it.
